The Know Your World Initiative Hot Topic Global Dialogues engages students in discussions on challenging and thought-provoking global issues, from the refugee crisis, to land disputes, to energy and the economy, using real-world country case studies.

Students log on to WorldVuze to join students around the world in a case study discussion by sharing perspectives on the Know Your World Initiative Dialogue questions, using only the information presented in the case study.
