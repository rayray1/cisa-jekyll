INTRODUCTION

The dynamic nature of International Relations within and without Kenya has presented opportunities and risks to the different actors that we have today. This environment provides an avenue of new issues to arise which ought to be addressed and explained. The Centre for International and Security Affairs (CISA) is an organization set out to share the view on these and many more issues through the eyes of the intellectuals. It is borne from the idea to engage their minds in the process of contributing to the development of local and international policies.

The involvement of the intellectuals from majors in Security and International Relations among other related disciplines provides a holistic approach to providing sustainable strategies towards contemporary Security and International Relations challenges experienced within the country, region and in the international system at large.

The organization is embedded on the principle “you always leave a place better than you found it”; we always seek solutions. It is divided into a three-fold framework, Research, Think Tank and consultancy. These three offer a wide platform to engage with individuals, other organizations on pertinent issues in the domestic and international environment.

THINK TANK

The Think Tank aspect is strengthened by the urge among students to contribute to the discourses in the field of International Studies along with the diverse interests among its members as well as affiliates. This characteristic of the organization provides a unique platform for different minds and backgrounds to bring about wide views and hence solutions to contemporary security and international relations challenges. There is also a strong need to make publications on a regular basis, this is so as to continually improve our knowledge on international and security affairs as well as grow to be relevant in the market.


The Consultancy is designed to give advisory opinion biased to international and security affairs to political parties, individuals, local Non-Governmental Organizations and not limited to also international organizations. This is supported by the knowledge on various disciplines such as

  • Diplomacy
  • Security Studies
  • Foreign Policy
  • International Relations
  • International Law
  • Strategic Studies and International Conflict Management

WHAT WE BELIEVE IN

We are of the view that one way of achieving global peace and security is through creation and sharing of knowledge from people from all walks of life


Objectives

  • To provide a platform for the youth to develop their intellectual capacity
  • To actively engage in international and security affairs policy formulation sphere
  • To nurture intellectuals through constructive discourse as well as promotion of research and scientific publications
